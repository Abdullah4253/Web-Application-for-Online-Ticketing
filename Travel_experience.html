Your security is our topmost priority. With the assistance of Road Master’s Eagle Eye Surveillance, travelling with us ensures that you and your family enjoy the journey in absence of any fear. Air Purification System

Free wifi

For the first time in Pakistan, enjoy Hybrid Ticketing System that gives you the advantage of early booking, changes with peak hours or off-peak hours and benefits of flexible fares that come with the position & placement of your seat in the bus.
