SAZ GROUP OF PAKISTAN

SAZ GROUP OF PAKISTAN started operations almost four decades ago, when the late Mr. Sheikh Muhammad Tufail along with his eldest son, Mr. Sheikh Javed Iqbal, laid the foundation stone for SAZ Sanitary Fittings in 1982 in Sialkot, one of Pakistan’s major industrial cities situated in the heart of Punjab.

The group has since then come to diversify into multiple business sectors, such as manufacturing, FMCG, B2B, transportation and logistics. Over the years, the SAZ Group has earned the trust and support of customers and industry peers alike. Our commitment to quality, workmanship and business ethics can be cited as one of the primary reasons why we have enjoyed our unique position in the market today.

SAZ CORPORATION’S JOURNEY

SAZ Group has been formed under SAZ Road Corporation with the aim to provide customers with the luxurious travel that they deserve. We further aim to reform intercity transportation in Pakistan by making customer journeys easier through technology and data.
